— About Tarang —

The Spectrum of Technology and Culture

Tarang is the annual tech and cultural extravaganza of Government Polytechnic College Kannur — a vibrant celebration where engineering brilliance meets artistic expression.

Across two electrifying days, students from all six departments showcase their innovations, compete in cutting-edge technical events, and light up the stage with cultural performances that embody the spirit of Kerala's finest polytechnic talent.
